About Sketyl

In case you didn't notice, Sketyl is a website. And yes, I know it looks terrible. It'll improve. Hopefully... Sketyl is a 100% open source project, and was designed with utmost speed and navigational simplicity as priorities. The complete source code can be found at https://github.com/Hecate946/Sketyl

Function

Sketyl shows you your favorite tracks and artists, what you've listened to recently, and provides audio previews for each song.
