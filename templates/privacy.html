Privacy Policy

Data Collection

Your Spotify data is fetched from Spotify via the web-api for the sole purpose of providing you with website functionality. Your Spotify username is stored in order to maintain your login state, and can be deleted at any time by disconnecting your account via the "Disconnect" button. No additional data of any kind is collected from you.

Data Storage

While you are logged in to Sketyl, your username is encrypted, and stored securely in a database. No additional data of any kind is permanently stored. I, Hecate946, have sole access to this database, and can be contacted with questions or concerns via email at Hecate946@gmail.com

Cookies

One session cookie, (a short unique text string), is stored in your browser to keep you logged in. This cookie is deleted upon account disconnection. No additional cookies, including Third-Party cookies, are used by this website. Nevertheless, cookies can be disabled and deleted at any time via your browser settings. Note: Some website features may not be fully functional without cookies.
